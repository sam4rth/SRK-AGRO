Our Story

SRK AGRO FOOD AND FUEL PRIVATE LIMITED was founded in 2020 in Nanded, Maharashtra, with a clear mission — to produce authentic, chemical-free jaggery powder that reflects the heritage and purity of rural India.

  • 100% natural, chemical-free jaggery production
  • Trusted by countless happy customers across India
  • Registered under MCA (CIN: U15490MH2020PTC348547)

Led by directors Shakuntala Raosaheb Kawale and Sindhuja Gunwant Kawale, our factory combines traditional food processing wisdom with modern hygiene practices. Every batch of jaggery we create is a step toward healthier communities and a sweeter future.
