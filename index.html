Our Products

From retail packs to wholesale solutions, our jaggery products are made to serve every kind of customer — with consistent quality, hygienic processing, and on-time delivery.

Jaggery Powder

Our jaggery powder is produced in a modern facility using traditional methods. Uniform granules, consistent quality, and zero added chemicals — ideal for retailers, bakeries, and wellness brands.

Bagasse

SRK’s processed bagasse is ideal for use in boilers, brick kilns, paper mills, and eco-friendly packaging. Available in bulk quantities with consistent moisture and fiber levels.

Molasses

SRK AGRO offers high-quality cane molasses suitable for alcohol distillation, cattle feed, and organic fertilizer production. Consistent viscosity and composition make it ideal for bulk buyers and agro-industrial clients.

Pressmud

SRK AGRO’s pressmud is collected under hygienic conditions and offered in bulk for use as organic manure, compost base, and biogas substrate. High in calcium, phosphates, and organic carbon — ideal for farms and agri-input manufacturers.
